~Service Packages~

· Traditional ·

Package Description:

This would consist of a word of welcome from your officiant, your vows and ring exchange, and your presentation as a married couple. This is for those who want a more traditional yet simple ceremony that offers couples the opportunity to infuse their own spiritual beliefs and personality to their ceremony.

Features:

  • Unlimited communication (in-person consults, phone calls, Skype, etc.)
  • Parchment copy of ceremony
  • Facilitate rehearsal

Fee: $200

If venue is over 75 miles from Mt. Jackson, VA, gas and lodging will be extra.
